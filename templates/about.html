The world generates at least 3.5 million tons of waste per day and this number is still increasing day by day that's why we need to aware about waste.
The main purpose of our project is to classify wastes with high performance.
In many countries people dispose of their waste without classifying it, so countries need to establish facilities to classify waste.
It is important for the economy of the country that these wastes are classified with high performance. Because the more accurate the classification of waste, the more they can contribute to their economy.
Classify

The prime motivation behind this project has been the problem that affects us all i.e Waste Management. The million tons of waste that are generated everyday, do not get properly segreagted and as a result large portion are mixed and are dumped in landmines. This ultimately piles up and their effect on the environment esp. soil and groundwater resources becomes devastating. Thus, we have come up with the idea that if we can segregate the waste items at the primary level itself, then the percentage of mixup can be reduced by a great margin. This in turn will also help in increasing the amount of recyclable items from the waste heap.
